Sunken Ships (that aren't the Titanic)

The RMS Titanic was not the only White Star Line ship to sink and cause tremendous loss of life. Here are a few more ships that sank while sailing under their flag.

  1. RMS Tayleur - 21 January 1854 - this unfortunate ship, she too sank on her maiden voyage.
  2. SS Atlantic - 1 April 1873 - the White Start Line's biggest recorded loss of life, prior to the Titanic that is.
